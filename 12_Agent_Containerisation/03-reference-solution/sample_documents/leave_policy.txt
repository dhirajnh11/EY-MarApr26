Leave Policy — Internal

Eligibility: All full-time employees are eligible for paid leave starting from their
date of joining. There is no waiting period.

Annual Leave: Every full-time employee is entitled to 24 working days of annual paid
leave per calendar year. Unused leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 12 days
into the following year.

Sick Leave: Employees are entitled to 10 days of sick leave per calendar year. Sick
leave of more than 2 consecutive days requires a medical certificate.

Casual Leave: 6 days per calendar year. Casual leave cannot be carried forward.

Parental Leave: Primary caregivers are eligible for 26 weeks of paid parental leave.
Secondary caregivers are eligible for 4 weeks of paid parental leave.

Leave Approval: All planned leave must be applied for at least 5 working days in
advance through the HR portal and requires approval from the reporting manager.

Unplanned absences must be communicated to the reporting manager by 9:30 AM on the
day of absence, either by phone or email.
