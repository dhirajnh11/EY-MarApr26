Expense Reimbursement Policy — Internal

Purpose: This policy describes how employees can claim reimbursement for business
expenses incurred on behalf of the company.

Eligible Expenses:
- Client-site travel (flights, trains, cab/taxi fares)
- Hotel accommodation during official travel
- Meals during official travel, subject to per diem limits
- Client entertainment with prior approval
- Internet and mobile reimbursement for remote employees, up to INR 1,500 per month

Per Diem Limits (within India):
- Metro cities: INR 2,000 per day
- Non-metro cities: INR 1,500 per day

Submission Process:
- All expense claims must be submitted through the finance portal within 30 days of
  the expense being incurred.
- Original receipts or scanned copies must be attached for every expense above INR 500.
- Claims without supporting receipts will be rejected.

Approval and Payment:
- Claims are reviewed by the reporting manager within 3 working days.
- Approved claims are reimbursed with the following month's salary cycle.

Non-Reimbursable: Personal expenses, alcohol (unless pre-approved for client
entertainment), and fines or penalties are not reimbursable under any circumstances.
