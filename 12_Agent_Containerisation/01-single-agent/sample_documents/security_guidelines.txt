Information Security Guidelines — Internal

Passwords:
- Minimum 12 characters, with a mix of upper case, lower case, digits, and symbols.
- Passwords must be rotated every 90 days.
- Reuse of the last 5 passwords is not allowed.
- Never share your password over email, chat, or phone.

Multi-Factor Authentication:
- MFA is mandatory for all corporate accounts (email, VPN, source control, cloud
  consoles).
- Use the approved authenticator app. Do not rely on SMS-based MFA where an
  authenticator app is available.

Device Security:
- Laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled.
- Screens must auto-lock after 5 minutes of inactivity.
- Do not install software from untrusted sources.

Data Handling:
- Classify data as Public, Internal, Confidential, or Restricted.
- Restricted data (financial records, customer PII, source code of client systems)
  must never be stored on personal devices or shared over personal accounts.

Incident Reporting:
- Report suspected phishing, malware, or data leaks to security@company.internal
  within 1 hour of discovery.
- Do not attempt to remediate suspected security incidents yourself.
